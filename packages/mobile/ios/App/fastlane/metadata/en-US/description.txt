trizum makes splitting bills with friends and family effortless. Whether you're sharing a dinner, planning a trip, or managing household expenses with roommates, trizum keeps track of who owes what.

KEY FEATURES

• Offline-First: Works without internet. Your expenses sync automatically when you're back online.

• Real-Time Sync: Share expenses instantly with your group. Everyone stays up to date.

• Smart Splitting: Split bills equally, by percentage, or by exact amounts. trizum calculates who owes whom.

• Receipt Attachments: Snap a photo of your receipt and attach it to any expense.

• Multiple Groups: Create separate groups for different occasions - trips, roommates, events, and more.

• Settlement Suggestions: Get optimized suggestions to settle debts with the fewest transactions.

• Privacy Focused: Your data stays on your devices. No accounts required to get started.

PERFECT FOR

• Roommates splitting rent and utilities
• Friends sharing dinner or drinks
• Group trips and vacations
• Couples managing shared expenses
• Event organizers tracking costs

trizum is free to use. Start splitting bills the smart way today!
