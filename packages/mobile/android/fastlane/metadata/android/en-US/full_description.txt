trizum makes splitting bills with friends and family effortless. Whether you're sharing a dinner, planning a trip, or managing household expenses with roommates, trizum keeps track of who owes what.

<b>KEY FEATURES</b>

• <b>Offline-First</b>: Works without internet. Your expenses sync automatically when you're back online.

• <b>Real-Time Sync</b>: Share expenses instantly with your group. Everyone stays up to date.

• <b>Smart Splitting</b>: Split bills equally, by percentage, or by exact amounts. trizum calculates who owes whom.

• <b>Receipt Attachments</b>: Snap a photo of your receipt and attach it to any expense.

• <b>Multiple Groups</b>: Create separate groups for different occasions - trips, roommates, events, and more.

• <b>Settlement Suggestions</b>: Get optimized suggestions to settle debts with the fewest transactions.

• <b>Privacy Focused</b>: Your data stays on your devices. No accounts required to get started.

<b>PERFECT FOR</b>

• Roommates splitting rent and utilities
• Friends sharing dinner or drinks
• Group trips and vacations
• Couples managing shared expenses
• Event organizers tracking costs

trizum is free to use. Start splitting bills the smart way today!
