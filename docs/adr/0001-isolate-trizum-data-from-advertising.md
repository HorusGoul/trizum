# Isolate trizum data from advertising

trizum will not provide expense, group, participant, or account data to
advertising systems for targeting, content mapping, creating publisher
identifiers, or any other purpose. AdMob may generate its own consent-governed,
pseudonymous Publisher First-Party ID, but trizum will not enrich it with
domain, account, or analytics data, user-insight surveys, custom identifiers,
or optional third-party identifier sharing. Keeping shared financial and social
context outside the advertising boundary preserves user trust and avoids
creating a sensitive-data dependency that would be difficult to reverse.
